5 HTML Tags

Here is a descripted list of five cool & knowledgeable HTML tags that I have learned thus far!

img
- inserts and shows an image on your webpage.
li
- a tag used to list items within your desired list type (ol,dl,ul).
strong
- a tag used to emphasise text.
hr
- a tag used to add a break between bodies of text.
b
- a tag used to make text bold.
